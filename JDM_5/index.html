The Legendary Rotary: Mazda RX-7

The Mazda-RX7 changed the game in the automotive world, not only in proffesional racing but the streets as well. Being one of the only cars ever produced with a rotary engine, the RX-7 and its rotary engine established themselves as pioneers of their time. Still highly regarded and sought after til this day, RX-7 empahizes this legacy.
