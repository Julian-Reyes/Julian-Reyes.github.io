About

Mission Statement

Dedicated to the upmost high-quality service and an unbreakable passion for automobiles, Express Auto Detail is your go-to for auto detailing service, with the best prices to stay competitive while still offering high-quality work, and just as important, only the best high-qualtiy products as well. Express Auto Detailers take pride in thier work, and have carefully experimented and researched to use only the BEST products available on the market. Our products are used by the best professionals worldwide, as they are the standard in exceptional-quality detailing industry.

History

With an arsenal of experience and a need for a more affordable mobile detailing service, Express Auto Detail was born in Sacramento, CA in 2019. With mutliple friends with their own detailing businesses of their own in the wealthy areas of Southern California, our noble founder had a hefty amount of experience in the auto detailing world where he gained valuable knowledge, skills, resources, and networks. Because of that, Express Auto Detail has expanded to Northern California to serve those in the Sacramento region.
